Overview

Project

FMV

The Lucid is a narrative-driven Full Motion Video (FMV) experience, currently in active development. The project blends live-action footage with interactive storytelling, player-driven choices, and internal state variables that influence both narrative direction and endings.

Production status

Work in progress

The project is currently in pre-production and system design. Scene breakdowns, variables, and narrative structure are being finalized before beginning the shooting.

Shooting schedule

Planned
  • Shooting period: May 2026 to September 2026
  • Location: Montreal, Canada
  • Expected time of production: September 2025 to December 2026
  • Director: Andrei Bituleanu

Release

Target

The game is planned for release on Steam by the end of 2026, or Q1 2027 at the latest.
